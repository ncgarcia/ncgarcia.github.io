I am mainly interested in computer vision and deep learning.
My current research is related to multimodal data, video understanding, and deep neural networks.
I am also broadly interested in data engineering and data science.

As of September 2020, I am with the [Department of Informatics](https://ciencias.ulisboa.pt/pt/di) in the Faculty of Sciences at the University of Lisbon as an Assistant Professor. I am also a member of the Management Committee of Cost Action ["CA19134 - Distributed Knowledge Graphs"](https://www.cost.eu/actions/CA19134/).

Previously, I was responsible for the undergrad Computer Graphics course at [University Lusófona](https://www.ulusofona.pt/).
Until 2020, I was a PhD Fellow in the [Pattern Analysis and Computer Vision Lab](https://pavis.iit.it) at the Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT).
My PhD (2016-2019) is with IIT and Università di Genova, advised by [Vittorio Murino](https://www.iit.it/people/vittorio-murino) and working with [Pietro Morerio](https://www.iit.it/people/pietro-morerio). During this period I spent six months at [Boston University's IVC Lab](https://www.bu.edu/cs/ivc/), hosted by [Stan Sclaroff](http://www.cs.bu.edu/~sclaroff/).
